WHAT THIS PROJECT DOES (Dumbed down):
Imagine you are selling SKF bearings on Amazon. You want to know if someone else starts selling the exact same bearing for cheaper, so you don't lose all your customers. Or maybe you want to know if a competitor runs out of stock in a specific city like Chennai, so you can raise your prices safely. 

This project is basically a robot that automatically behaves like a real human buyer from different cities in India. 
1. It searches Amazon for SKF bearings.
2. It looks at every single seller offering that bearing.
3. It figures out who Amazon is promoting as the "Buy Now" winner in specific cities (because Amazon changes this based on where the buyer lives).
4. It catches fake prices and secret coupon codes to figure out the *true* net price.
5. It throws all this data into a sleek, dark-mode tracking dashboard so you can visualize exactly how your competitors are behaving, where they are winning, and alert you instantly the second someone undercuts your price!
